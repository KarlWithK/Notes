However, on May 4, 1970, four students were killed by the Ohio state troops who acted "self-defense". This led to the NYC Mayor at the time, Mayor John Lindsay to cast the American flag City Hall at half-mast to respect the death of the students. In addition to this, many students in NYC started to also participate in a United Nations protest. In reaction to both of these events, construction workers who proclaimed their selves as "Vietnam tartans" would protest to that protest.
