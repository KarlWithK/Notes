Loseless Vs Lossy

  • Lossless compression algorithms contain enough information to exactly reacreate the original image.
    • PNG (Portable Newtwork Graphics), GIF (Graphics Interchanfe Format)
    • Made a hash table of patterns that show up
  • Lossy Compression algorithms do not retain all the details of the original image.
    • JPEG (Joint Photographic Experts Group)
    • Smooth files but can't bring back

PPM (Portable PixMap)

  • Uncompressed raster format.
  • Pixel data is representeed by RGB triplets in either ASCII or binary. You can read it.
  • All whitespace is equivalent.
