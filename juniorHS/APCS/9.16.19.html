    Boolean operations, named in honor of George Boole, are operations that manipulated values of true and false: 1 for true 0 for false

    And: only if all is true

    or: if one is true Nary

    xor: both inputs are different

    Not: opposite.

    Satisfies: Commutative, Associative, distributive, identity, implementation, Demo gen

    Look at y for Boolean values. (X ^ Y) v (X ^ Y) = (XY) v (BY) or X`Y v BY

    XY v XY = Y(X v X) = Y (1) = Y

    Gates: A device that produces the output of a Boolean operation is called a gate. Gates provide the building blocks to create circuits.
