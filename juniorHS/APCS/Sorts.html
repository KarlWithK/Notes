Merge sort Algorithm [O (n log n)]

MergeSort(arr[], i, r)

if r > i

  1. FInd the middle point to divide the array into two halves: middle m = (i + r) /2
  2. Call mergeSort for first half: Call mergeSort(arrm i, m)
  3. Call mergeSort for second half: Call mergeSort*arr, m+1, r)
  4. Merge the two halves sorted in step 2 and 3: Call merge(arr, i, m, r)

Into the 'merged' list, until we have no more elements in either list. Append any leftover elements to the list.

for example: 38, 27, 43, 3, 9, 82, 10
