Why Machines That Bend Are Better https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=97t7Xj_iBv0

  1. Compliant mechanisms are mechanisms that bend. Orginally considered bad to have flexibility in your machines.
  2. Like other devices, you can still use these mechanisms to achieve the same amount of force.
  3. Compliant mechanisms have a number of advantages over traditional mechanisms like: low part count, Simple production processes, lower price, precise motion, performance, proportions, portable and predictable.
  4. Compliant mechanisms have a low number of parts instead of hinges and other parts.
  5. Thanks to low part count you can easily make them at a cheap price. And move the parts around
  6. You can use mechanisms from other industries and make these device with precise motion and don't suffer from backlash, as such they have better performance overall.
  7. Since the design is almost the same you can just scale it up and have different proportions.
  8. The simple design also makes it more portable and perfect for space applications.
  9. These mechanisms can be used with rigid materials and use other ideas to make it better.
  10. Compliant Mechanisms allow a design to be used as a safeguard for nuclear weapons because it is predictable.
