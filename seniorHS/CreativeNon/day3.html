The Undress Code Bonnie Tsui

What interested me was the title, and thankfully it got me somewhere. I liked the way Tsui was able to tell a powerful political message by telling her own story and it sticks. She is a writer herself, and she was able to stand up against this creepy man. I feel like the best thing she did was to include the creepy comments the man said, but not directly say he was creepy.
