  • Representatives and Sentators:
    • 535 Members, 435 in the House, 100 in the Senate
    • House members must be at least 25 years old and American citizens for 7 years, 2 years terms
    • Senators must be at least 30 and citizens for 9 years, 6 years terms, 3 classes.
    • Members are more likely to be rich, old and white
    • Descriptive V Substantive representative
      • How well can the rich represent the poor, and whites present blacks, etc.
    • underrepresentation of Women:
      • Less Likely to run
      • Face some discrimination when doing so.

      • Time managed intensely

    • The senate:

      • 3rd in line for the presidency

      • Opposed by the minority leader (McCarthy)
